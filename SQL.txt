CREATE UNIQUE INDEX username ON users (username);


'id' - to uniquely identify each transaction.
'user_id' - to link the transaction to a specific user.
'symbol' - to store the stock symbol of the purchased stock.
'shares' - to store the number of shares purchased.
'price' - to store the price per share at the time of the transaction.
'timestamp' - to store when the transaction occurred.
Remember, the 'id' could be an auto-incrementing integer,
and 'user_id' would likely be a foreign key linking to the
'users' table. The 'symbol' could be a text field, 'shares'
could be an integer, 'price' could be a real number, and
'timestamp' could be a datetime field.
