To calculate the aura by averaging energy, valence & danceability of top
100 songs might not be very accurate because:
    Suppose that the user only ever listened to 100 songs in that year.
    Suppose that he listened to song 1 30 times & song 100 3 times
    Suppose song 1 & song 100 have polar characteristics
    In that case the average of song 1 & song 100 will cancel out, while in reality
    song 1 outweights song 100 by ratio 10:1. This could happen to all the other songs
    as well. Therefore a better approach would be to multiply the characteristics of
    each song by the number of times they were played & then average all the songs.
    For this, an additional data is needed, which is how many times each song was
    played or even better, for what total duration each song was played.
